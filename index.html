Localization Project Management System

Localization Project Management System (LPMS) is a web based platform for small and medium scale enterprises (SMEs), who cannot afford to purchase high-end systems for their day to day localization requirements.

It is “One for All” solution, which will bring all the translators, evaluators, proof-readers, reviewers, localization professionals, linguists and computation experts on the single platform.

Live Demonstration

In order to use the live demonstration of this system, kindly visit http://tdil-dc.in/lpms for more details. You may also find tutorials and audio-visuals of various user modules therein in "Tutorials" section.
